Gaussian quadrature

\[ \int_{-1}^1 f(x)\ \mathrm{d}x \approx \sum_{i=1}^n w_if(x_i) \] \[ \int_a^b f(x)\ \mathrm{d}x = \frac{b-a}{2}\int_{-1}^1 f\left(\frac{b-a}{2}\xi + \frac{a+b}{2}\right)\ \mathrm{d}\xi \]

Singular

\[ \int_{\partial\Omega} f(x,y)\ \mathrm{d}S_y = \int_{\partial\Omega} k(x,y)/(r^\beta(x,y))\ \mathrm{d}S_y \]

  1. \(\beta = 0\): Regular

  2. \(0 < \beta < 2\): Weakly singular

  3. \(\beta = 2\): Strongly singular

  4. \(2 < \beta \leq 3\): Hyper singular

  5. \(3 < \beta \): Super-singular

Regular and weakly singular boundary integrals are finite for a finite region problem. Strongly, hyper-singular, and super-singular boundary integrals are finite only if \(k(x,y)\) satisfies certain conditions.
