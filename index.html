About me

I'm currently a PhD student sitting in the Acoustic Technology Group (ACT) as well as The Centre for Acoustic-Mechanical Micro Systems (CAMM) at The Technical University of Denmark. My project deals with Efficient Numerical Optimization for Acoustics with Viscous and Thermal Losses and its Application to Acoustic Micro-Devices. In short we aim to accelerate the boundary element method including losses using hiarchical matrices.
