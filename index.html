About me

I'm currently a PhD student sitting in the Acoustic Technology Group (ACT) as well as The Centre for Acoustic-Mechanical Micro Systems (CAMM) at The Technical University of Denmark.

My project is titled Efficient Numerical Optimization for Acoustics with Viscous and Thermal Losses and its Application to Acoustic Micro-Devices.

The overall goal of the project is to accelerate the computational efficiency of the the boundary element method for acoustical computations including losses. We expect that this can achieved by utilizing hiarchical matrices and the fast multipole method.
